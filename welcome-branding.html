folder overview

Your Branding Client Portal folder is your shared space for everything we're working on together at Lindsay Dev Creative. Here's a quick overview of what you'll find inside.

01Admin

Your business paperwork lives here, including contracts, agreements, invoices, receipts, proposals, and SOWs. Anytime you need to reference something official tied to the project, check here.

02Brand Assets

This is where your brand lives. Drop any materials here: logos, fonts, color specs, photography, past marketing assets. You can drag and drop from your desktop, or use the + New button in Drive to upload.

Once your branding project wraps, your final logo files, color palette, and typography system will also be delivered here, ready to use anywhere your brand lives.

If you have questions or need a file you're not seeing, just reach out through the Communication Hub in your Client Portal. I'm excited to work together!
